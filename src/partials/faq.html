FAQ

  • Is the game free to play?

    Yes! Tiles Tap: Triple Tile Match is free to download and play. Optional in-app purchases are available for hints, power-ups, and visual themes

  • How do I earn coins?

    Coins are awarded after completing levels, as well as through daily log-ins, events, and watching optional ads. Coins are used to buy hints and unlock custom tile sets

  • Are there ads in the game?

    The game includes minimal, non-intrusive ads. You can also opt for an ad-free version through a one-time purchase

  • Can I sync my progress across devices?

    Yes, if you connect the game to your Google Play account, your progress will be saved and can be restored on any supported device

  • What platforms is the game available on?

    It is currently available on Android devices (version 5.1 and up). Support for iOS may be added in the future

  • How do I play the game?

    Simply tap on three identical tiles to make them disappear. The goal is to clear the entire board. You can only stack up to 7 tiles in your tray — if it fills without a match, you lose the round

  • How often is new content added?

    We release new levels and features monthly, including fresh visual themes and community event content. Updates are automatic via Google Play

  • What is Zen Mode?

    Zen Mode allows you to play endlessly with no failure conditions. There are no move limits or objectives — just peaceful tile-matching for your mental reset

  • Where can I report a bug or suggest a feature?

    You can contact our support team directly via the contact email at the bottom of this page. We welcome feedback and try to reply within 48 hours

  • How many levels are in the game?

    The game currently includes over 400 levels with more being added regularly. Difficulty scales gradually to ensure you're always challenged but never overwhelmed

  • Can I play offline?

    Absolutely. The game is fully functional without an internet connection, making it perfect for travel or quiet offline sessions
